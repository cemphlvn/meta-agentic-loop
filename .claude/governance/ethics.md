## What Are Ethics?

Ethics define the moral boundaries of the system. They answer:
- **What is right and wrong for this project?**
- **What lines will never be crossed?**
- **How do we handle ethical dilemmas?**

Ethics are absolute. They cannot be overridden by efficiency, profit, or convenience.
